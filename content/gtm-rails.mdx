This page is the guardrail for anything outward-facing: the approved way to describe the Arm Care Intelligence System (ACIS), the language we never use, and the live status of partner conversations.

## Language discipline

A CI lint (`scripts/lint-language.mjs`) blocks banned framing from every live page of this knowledge base, so the build itself enforces the rule. The banned list and its rationale are documented in CLAUDE.md at the repo root.

Two standing rules beyond the lint:

- ACIS is a post-game system and is always described that way. It is never described as a real-time or in-game system.
- Outbound material may only use facts flagged `outbound: true` in `src/lib/canonical.ts`. Pages marked `audience: outbound` enforce this at build time and automatically use plain-language labels where they exist (for example, the AUC renders as its approved plain-language label).
